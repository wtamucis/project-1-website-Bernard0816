About Bernard

I focus on practical web development—structuring pages with semantic HTML, styling with CSS, and keeping everything version-controlled with Git. My goal is to produce professional pages that are easy to read and easy to maintain.

Beyond web dev, I’m exploring cybersecurity tools like Wireshark, Nessus, and Nmap. I enjoy turning complex topics into clear, helpful write-ups and documenting what I learn so others can follow along.

I also think about product ideas (like my SHENGO concept) and how design, accessibility, and performance come together to create a great user experience.
